FAQ

Common questions.

Is this just another Slack clone? +

No. Slack is a communication layer. MARKHUB is the execution layer that sits on top. In Slack, tasks only exist if someone manually creates them. In MARKHUB, the AI reads every conversation and creates them automatically.

How is this different from Otter or Fireflies? +

Otter and Fireflies record calls and give you a transcript. MARKHUB is a full workspace where conversation capture is just one channel. The output isn't a document — it's a task list ready to execute, inside the same app where your team works.

Does a bot join our calls? +

No bot joins. MARKHUB captures audio directly from your device — invisible to everyone else on the call. When the conversation ends, tasks appear in your Data Bucket automatically.

We already use Slack and Notion. Do we have to switch? +

MARKHUB can run alongside your existing tools. Most teams find they gradually stop needing them — MARKHUB already has chat, tasks, notes, calendar, and files in one place, with tasks that create themselves.

Is there a free plan? +

Yes. Free includes up to 5 members, 100 Chat AI extractions, and 5 Meeting AI calls per month — no credit card required. Pro ($19/mo) unlocks unlimited members, unlimited Chat AI, and 100 Meeting AI calls. Business ($99/mo) scales further with 300 calls, SSO, and priority support. Enterprise is custom-priced.

Common questions
What counts as a "Meeting AI" call?+
Any audio or video call processed through MARKHUB — phone calls via Markly, in-app group calls, or uploaded recordings. Each call counts as 1 regardless of duration. A 5-minute standup and a 90-minute investor call both use 1 credit.
Why is member count unlimited on paid plans?+
We think paying per seat is the wrong model for a tool built around team conversations. The more teammates you add, the more conversations happen — and the more value Meeting AI creates. You pay for AI usage, not headcount. Adding a new hire should feel free.
What is a "hub"?+
A hub is a self-contained workspace for a project, client, or team — chat, tasks, calendar, notes, and files all in one view. Think of it as a Slack channel that comes with a full project workspace built in. On Free you get 1 hub, Pro gives you 3, and Business unlocks unlimited.
Can I try before paying?+
Yes. The Free plan is free forever with no credit card required — 5 members, 1 hub, 5 Meeting AI calls per month. Pro and Business both include a 14-day free trial with full access. Cancel anytime, no questions asked.
What's the difference between Chat AI and Meeting AI?+
Chat AI reads your text conversations in real time and extracts tasks automatically — it's always on and unlimited on paid plans. Meeting AI is the heavy lifter: it transcribes recorded calls, generates summaries, and extracts structured tasks from spoken conversations. This is where the real time savings come from, and it's what we meter for billing.
What is the AI Agent and what can it do right now?+
The AI Agent (MAKi) currently handles task creation from conversations and in-hub research — finding references, summarizing links, and surfacing relevant context. Proactive execution (sending emails, wiring payments, booking meetings on your behalf) is in active development and will roll out to Pro and Business first.
Can I add more Meeting AI calls if I hit the limit?+
Yes. On Pro, additional calls are $0.50 each. On Business, $0.30 each. These are charged at the end of the billing cycle. There's no hard cutoff — calls keep working and overages appear on your next invoice. Enterprise plans include unlimited calls with no overage fees.
Is my conversation data used to train AI models?+
No. Your hub conversations, call transcriptions, and task data are never used to train AI models — ours or anyone else's. On Business and Enterprise plans, you can also opt for on-premise processing so data never leaves your infrastructure.
